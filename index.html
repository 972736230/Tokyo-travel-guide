About Us

Tokyo Media Center is responsible for the daily operations of Go! Tokyo, a multi-media information platform comprising websites and social media. We currently have 50 editors and a total of 100 staff members who work together to produce a newsletter about Tokyo's food, travel, and culture.

You can find all you are interested in and need to know about Tokyo here: customs, traditions, tourist attractions, hotels, restaurants, travel, nightlife, and shopping.Whether you are a visitor to Tokyo from the past or have lived in Tokyo for a long time, Go!Tokyo. can provide you with the information you need.
